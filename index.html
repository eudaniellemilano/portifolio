About me

Data as language,
strategy as outcome.

Data analyst with a background in marketing and an ongoing technical postgraduate in Data Science at FIAP — where my team is among the finalists of NEXT 2025. My work spans data analysis, engineering and strategy.

I work with SQL for complex and optimized queries, Python for statistical analysis and modeling, and databases like BigQuery and PgAdmin. I apply methodologies such as A/B testing, RFM segmentation and K-Means to identify patterns and support decisions.

In digital instrumentation, I structure event tracking via Google Tag Manager and GA4, ensuring data quality that feeds funnel, engagement and conversion analyses.

I bring insights directly to executive teams, build revenue and cost projection models, and actively participate in data-driven strategic planning.
